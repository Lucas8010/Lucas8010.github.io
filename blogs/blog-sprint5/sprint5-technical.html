I also definitely learned on how to program with others pretty good too, I did the minesweeper and the js carnival with one of the cohorts members and it was a joy to do it with them too, I appreciated every single second I spent with them.

another one that I learned was how to work or focus in a more efficient way, I used to be distraced by the most tiniest thing but now the only thing that could ruin my learning is either, people talking loudly or calling my name from across the room, I also have got a bit better via trying to study using music.
